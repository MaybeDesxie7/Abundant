ABOUT US

ABUNDANT FRUITS is a fruit, nut, and spice distributor in Southern Africa. We export high-quality agricultural products from Zimbabwe and some Southern African countries to wholesalers around the world.

Abundant Fruits was established to provide high-quality, nutritious, and delicious tropical fruits, nuts, and spices from Zimbabwe and neighboring countries to global markets at a reasonable price. At Abundant Fruits, we understand that ‚Äúthere is enough for everyone‚Äù; thus, Abundant Fruits works on the benefits of not only its shareholders but also customers, local farmers, and all other related parties.

Currently, Abundant Fruits is expanding to reach more customers worldwide. By the end of 2022, Zimbabwean fruits and vegetables were reaching only about 10 markets globally, while the potential is still much greater.

We combine modern post-harvest handling with ethical sourcing to protect both product quality and local communities.

Product Lines

Fresh Fruits

A colorful range of seasonal fruits harvested at peak ripeness and delivered with care.

Our fresh fruits include avocados, mangoes, bananas, citrus, and table grapes sourced from trusted farms across Zimbabwe and Southern Africa. Each fruit is hand-picked, sorted, and packed under strict quality standards to preserve natural flavor, nutrition, and freshness for export markets.

Processed Fruits

Convenient fruit options tailored for retail and industrial use.

We supply dried fruits, frozen fruits, purees, and concentrates made from farm-fresh produce. Processing is done under internationally certified facilities, ensuring long shelf-life while retaining natural taste, nutrients, and vibrant colors. Ideal for food manufacturers, retailers, and wholesalers.

Nuts

High-quality nuts carefully selected for taste, grade, and nutrition.

Our nut product line includes raw and roasted peanuts, cashews, macadamias, and other regional specialties. They are graded by size and quality, then processed with care to preserve flavor and health benefits. Perfect for snacks, cooking, and food production industries.

Spices & Condiments

Aromatic spices and condiments that bring authentic flavors to every dish.

We offer ginger, turmeric, chili, black pepper, and other natural spices sourced directly from growers. Carefully dried, ground, or blended to maintain purity, aroma, and potency. Our spices meet international safety standards and come in bulk or packaged formats for global distribution.
